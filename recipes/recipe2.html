Chicken Curry

Long long ago, the land of great spices and exquisite flavours, gave birth to the pinnacle of all chicken curries- The INDIAN Chicken Curry

Indian cooking primarily relies on the preparation for the dish. Take care to acquire best of the ingredients.

INGREDIENTS-

  • Chicken cuts - 1/2 kg
  • Onions - 4 to 5
  • Tomatoes - 4 to 5
  • Ginger garlic paste - 2 cups
  • Mustard oil - 4 tbsps
  • Yogurt - 3 to 4 cups
  • Turmeric powder - 2 tbsps
  • Red chilli powder - 3 to 4 tbsps
  • Mustard seeds - 1 to 2 tbsps
  • Cumin - 1 to 2 tbsps
  • Lime - half a slice
  • Chicken masala - to your spice tolerance
  • Coriander seeds powder - 3 cups
  • salt - to taste
Steps to follow :-
  1. The music of Marinade -

    • Take a cup and add yogurt, 2 tbsps of red chilli powder, turmeric 1/2 tbsps and some salt.
    • Mix the mixture well, till you get a consistent texture.
    • Add this yogurt mixture to the chicken. And massage the meat well and squeeze the lemon in as well.
    • Leave it at room temperature for at least a hour. The more time you leave it, more the acidic flavour gives a zing to the chicken.

  2. The oratory of the Pots and flames-

    • Place a pot or wok over the stove.
    • Add mustard oil to it, let it heat. And then add cumin and mustard seeds for it to roast.
    • Add finely diced onions to the hot oil (you must hear a sizzle)in medium flame and cook till they are slightly brown.
    • Add ginger garlic paste and stir it well. Be careful not to burn the onions or the paste. cook for 3 minutes.
    • Add salt, red chilli powder and turmeric and mix them. And finally add diced tomatoes to the pot.

  3. The chicken rush -

    • The pot must have some watery consistency till now because of the tomatoes watering down. Add marinated chicken pieces to the pot.
    • let it sit for a few minutes and then add the coriander seed powder and chicken masala. Stir well.
    • Add water if you would like more gravy for your curry. COver the pot and let it cook till the chicken is cooked.
    • Loose the flames and let the pot cool.


  4. Plates and appetite

    • Eat the curry with rice or chapati or naan.
    • And definitely share your food with your close ones.
